Infrastructure


Secretary K.Santhanam Block

  • The imposing Main Block named `SECRETARY K.SANTHANAM BLOCK has three floors covering an area of 77,000 sq. ft
  • It accommodates class rooms, tutorial rooms, seminar halls, laboratories for the Electrical Department, Electronics and Communication Department and Instrumentation and Control Engineering Department.

RV Block & Kamaraj Block

  • The RV Block covering an area of 76,500 sq. ft
  • It accommodates class rooms, tutorial rooms, seminar halls, and Common laboratories for the Computer Science and Engineering Department, Information Technology Department, First Year common courses and it also accommodates administrative offices.

Jagadguru Sri Jayendra Saraswathi Block

  • The Jagadguru Sri Jayendra Saraswathi block covering an area of 25,300 sq. ft
  • It houses the Civil Department and its Laboratories, Library and Conference Hall.

Poojyasri Rajaji Block


  • The Poojyasri Rajaji block covering an area of 25,300 sq. ft
  • It houses the Department of Management Studies, General Library, Digital Library & Conference Hall.

Mechanical Workshop

  • The Mechanical workshop covering an area of 21,600 sq. ft
  • It accommodates class rooms, tutorial rooms, seminar halls, and laboratories for the Mechanical Department.

Annadurai Iyer Mechanical Labs

  • The Annadurai Iyer Mechanical Labs covering an area of 10,400 sq. ft
  • It accommodates Laboratories for the Mechanical Department.

Common Workshop

  • The Workshop is covering an area of 10,100 sq. ft
  • It accommodates First Year Common Course Laboratories and Mechanical Department Laboratories.

Boys Hostel

  • The Boys Hostel covering an area of 40,400 sq. ft
  • It is equipped with Play Ground, Computer Center, and Wi-Fi Facility.
  • It consists of 40KVA Power Generator Facility.
  • Boys hostel rules and regulation

Girls Hostel

  • Mini computer center with 10 Mbps internet connectivity and Wi-Fi connectivity
  • 24-Hour all round security service to ensure safety of inmates.
  • Pure drinking water from RO plant.
  • It consists of 125KVA Power Generator Facility.
  • Girls Hostel rules and regulation

Generator Room

  • The Generator Room covering an area of 3,300 sq. ft
  • It consists of 625KVA and 140KVA Power Generator Facility.

Canteen

  • The Canteen covering an area of 8,350 sq. ft
  • It consist of separate dinning halls for both boys and girls.

Shed

  • The Shed covering an area of 17,100 sq. ft
  • Combination of Three Parking shed, exclusively meant both Students and Staff.

Security Room

  • The Security Room covering an area of 300 sq. ft


 SCE

Saranathan College of Engineering was founded by “Vidya Seva Ratnam”, “Guru Seva Mani” Auditor Sri. K. Santhanam in the year 1998. It was so named in respectful memory of his Guru Principal Saranathan, the greatest ever Principal of National College. It is a self financing college approved by AICTE and affiliated to Anna University, Chennai.
