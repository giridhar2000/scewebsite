Department Seminar Hall


IT department has a well-furnished adequately spacious, well ventilated and air-conditioned Seminar Hall. To assist the students to learn more effectively, Information and Communication Technology (ICT) mode of education is provided using the hardware and software resources available in the seminar hall. Hardware resources such as Projector, Computer, latest Audio-visual aids, Digital Touch Pen Pad, Digital Cameras, White Board etc are available and software applications such as EDMODO, GOOGLE CLASSROOM etc. are provisioned as well. The department invites distinguished academicians and industry experts to address the students and staff on recent technical topics every so often to widen the horizon of knowledge. Research meetings and peer enablement programmes are also conducted to enrich the knowledge of the staff. Technical events in various academic domains are organized for the students to bring out their potentials and for the overall development of the students.








 SCE

Saranathan College of Engineering was founded by “Vidya Seva Ratnam”, “Guru Seva Mani” Auditor Sri. K. Santhanam in the year 1998. It was so named in respectful memory of his Guru Principal Saranathan, the greatest ever Principal of National College. It is a self financing college approved by AICTE and affiliated to Anna University, Chennai.
