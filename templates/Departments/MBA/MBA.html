Department of MBA

Vision

To mould the college in to a top class National/International Centre of advanced learning ,technology & research and enhance national human resource and its power.

Mission

To Achieve excellence in business education, by adapting to the needs and challenges of dynamic and globally competitive business environment.



Unique Features


  • Incepted in the year 2002.

  • 18 Batches have emerged successfully with several University rank holders

  • MBA Programme approved by AICTE and Affiliated to Anna University,Chennai.

  • The Present sanctioned intake is 60 and it is a 2 years full time Programme.


    • Programme Overview

    MBA programme in Saranathan provides a comprehensive learning & exciting experience to gain and augment the necessary skills for this contemporary business scenario.

    The Broad MBA curriculum provides an outline to


  • Build Professionalism among MBA students.

  • To Excel in Finance, Marketing, Operations and Human Resource Specializations.

  • To Synchronize with Modern Management Concepts.

  • To Transform the theoretical and practical orientations into a Successful Management model.

  • To Impart Knowledge and to award an armoury of skills to the budding managers.


    • Programme Design

    This is a two-year full-time Programme, which follows semester pattern of Anna University, Chennai. The first year consists of core courses. The second year consists of a few core courses and several elective courses. A summer project is scheduled at the end of the first year. The programme offers dual specializations in the second year. A Main Project Work is scheduled at IV Semester.To enhance the hidden skills of the students a Practical subject entitled " Creativity and Innovation” is offered.

 SCE

Saranathan College of Engineering was founded by “Vidya Seva Ratnam”, “Guru Seva Mani” Auditor Sri. K. Santhanam in the year 1998. It was so named in respectful memory of his Guru Principal Saranathan, the greatest ever Principal of National College. It is a self financing college approved by AICTE and affiliated to Anna University, Chennai.
