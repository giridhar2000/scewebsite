FORMATION OF RESEARCH CELLS

The research cell in EEE was formed in July 2013 with an objective to enhance,to an appreciable level, the depth in understanding of engineering concepts,enabling the students and faculty to easily apply their academic learning to solve real time engineering problems and consequently develop solutions to cater to the needs of the society from an engineering perspective. As a result of continuous effort of the department, the Research Cell was officially recognized in August 2013 by Anna University Chennai and has been operating as one of its Centers for Research . The Research cell is headed by the HOD of the department..



Self Supporting Projects


Apart from the projects funded by the external funded agencies, the department has taken up various research projects with the support of institution, in the area of power quality and renewable energy to cater the societal needs and further strengthen the consultancy services offered by the department.



Other Ongoing Research Work Funded by Instituion

  • Performance Evaluation of 10 KW on grid and 1 KW off grid PV system

  • Economic Energy Management through Auditing

  • Power Quality Solution for Small Scale Industries

  • Power Quality Analysis for Renewable Systems

  • Value Engineering Analysis on Solar Water Heater

  • Energy Conservation in Waste Water Treatment through Green Energy Technology

 SCE

Saranathan College of Engineering was founded by “Vidya Seva Ratnam”, “Guru Seva Mani” Auditor Sri. K. Santhanam in the year 1998. It was so named in respectful memory of his Guru Principal Saranathan, the greatest ever Principal of National College. It is a self financing college approved by AICTE and affiliated to Anna University, Chennai.
