Vision

  • The Programme has been started with the noble vision of producing innovative Engineers with social consciousness

  • who can richly contribute towards creating a more ecological environment and enrich them in technological advancementsfuture.


Mission

  • To provide essential knowledge and skill to the students and enhance their employability potential and entrepreneurial capabilities.

  • To produce graduates of highly Professional and Ethical standards.

  • To produce knowledge base and consultancy services to the community in all areas of Civil Engineering.

  • To promote innovative and original thinking in the minds of budding Engineers to face the challenges of future.

PROGRAMME EDUCATIONAL OBJECTIVES - PEOs

  • To prepare the students for successful careers in Civil Engineering field that meets the standards of Indian and Multinational Companies.

  • To develop confidence and ability among students to synthesize data and technical concepts to apply it in real world problems.

  • To provide students with a sound foundation in Mathematical, Scientific, and Engineering fundamentals necessary to formulate solve and analyse engineering problems and strengthen foundations for graduate studies.

  • To promote students to work collaboratively on Multi-Disciplinary projects and make them engage in life-long learning process throughout their professional life.


PROGRAMME EDUCATIONAL OBJECTIVES - PEOs

  • To accomplish an elevated level of Technical Expertise to shine in Higher Education / Profession by attaining knowledge in Basic Sciences, Design and Drawing and Engineering Principles.

  • To explore and relate Modern Engineering Tools for Planning, Design, Execution and Maintenance of works that is Technically feasible, economically and socially acceptable.

  • To enhance Good Communication Skills, Team work in their responsibilities with excellence and to be prepared to face challenges in all occasions.


Progamme Outcomes

  • Capability to apply knowledge of mathematics, science and engineering.

  • Capability to propose and conduct experiments, as well as to evaluate and understand data in civil engineering.

  • Capability to design a system, component, or process to meet desired needs within economic, environmental, social, political, ethical, health and safety, manufacturability and sustainability.

  • Capability to task with multidisciplinary teams.

  • Capability to recognize, make, and clarify civil engineering crisis.

  • Ability to take up professional and ethical responsibility.

  • Capability to correspond professionally.

  • Capability to understand the impact of engineering solutions in a global, economic, environmental and societal context.

  • Ability to engage in life-long learning.

  • Capability of accepting current issues.

  • Capability to employ the talent, technique and contemporary engineering tools.

  • Capability to apply the engineering and management principles to one’s individual work and to supervise the projects as a member and team leader.

 SCE

Saranathan College of Engineering was founded by “Vidya Seva Ratnam”, “Guru Seva Mani” Auditor Sri. K. Santhanam in the year 1998. It was so named in respectful memory of his Guru Principal Saranathan, the greatest ever Principal of National College. It is a self financing college approved by AICTE and affiliated to Anna University, Chennai.
