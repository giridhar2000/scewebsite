CIVIL LABORATORY


SURVEYING LABORATORY

The Surveying Laboratory enables students to understand and practice the basic principles of surveying by conducting field exercises using a wide spectrum of surveying instruments. . This laboratory covers data collection methods including surveying, profile leveling, contouring, compass traversing, laying out of curves, base line measurement and triangulation. The Lab sessions would include experiments on Chain Surveying, Chain Traverse, Compass Surveying, Compass survey Traversing, Plane Table Surveying-- Radiation, intersection, Traverse, Resection Leveling, Dumpy Level/ Auto level Surveying, Surveying using Total Station.


Instruments: Traditional Dumpy levels, Tilting level, Compass, Transits, Plane table and Theodolites to the latest Electronic Auto level, Digital Theodolite, Total Station and GPS.

SOIL MECHANICS LABORATORY

The student will gain hands-on experience in conducting soil laboratory tests to determine soil parameters needed for Geotechnical Engineering Design, and to communicate this information with others. Further the laboratory involves determination of Moisture Content, Density, Liquid Limit, Plastic Limit, Shrinkage Limit, Bearing Capacity, Grain Size Distribution and Specific Gravity of Soil.


Equipments: Oven, Consolidometer, Shear Box, CBR, Compaction Testing Machine, Core-Cutter, Casagrande Apparatus, Test kit for Atterberg Limits.


STRENGTH OF MATERIALS TESTING LABORATORY

Material testing laboratory helps for selecting and verifying materials and to evaluate material quality, performance, troubleshooting, research and many other applications.


Equipments: Spring Testing Machine, Rockwell, Brinell Hardness Testing Machines, Torsion Testing Machine, Impact Tesing Machine, Universal Testing Machine, Deflection Test Apparatus, Rosette Strain Gauge Setup.



HYDRAULICS ENGINEERING LABORATORY

This lab is to teach students, about the understanding of Fluid Properties, Fluid Characteristics and Calibrate the Instruments based on Bernoulli’s Theorem. The experiments here are designed to demonstrate the applications of the basic fluid mechanics principles like Reynold’s Number, Frictional Losses, etc. This lab helps to gain knowledge on working of Centrifugal Pumps, Positive Displacement Pumps, Hydraulic Turbines. Students will compare the performance of various machines at different operating points.


Equipments: Orifice Meter& Venturimeter, Bernoulli’s Apparatus, Losses in pipes, Reciprocting Pump, Centrifugal Pump, Gear Pump, Jet Pump, Submersible Pump, Pelton Turbine, Francis Turbine, Kaplan Turbine, Pitot Tube, Pipe Friction, Turbo Impulse Wheel.


CONCRETE & HIGHWAY ENGINEERING LABORATORY

Concrete lab enables students to carry various experiments regarding Compressive Strength of Concrete, Tensile Strength of Concrete, Workability Test, Design Mix, Modulus of Rupture of Concrete. All these experiments intended to determine the quality of concrete.

Equipments: Vibrating Table, Compaction Factor Test apparatus, Slump Cone, Compression Testing Machine.

Highway Enginering lab is used for research and teaching in the fields of Rigid and Flexible Pavements and testing pavements materials and structures. Several instruments exist in the lab for conducting materials characterization tests.

Equipments: Los Angel Apparatus, Bitumen Extractor, Ductility Testing Machine, Bitumen Mixer, Bitumen Penetration Apparatus, Impaction Test Apparatus, Marshal Stability Test Apparatus.

WATER & WASTEWATER ANALYSIS LABORATORY

This Laboratory presents environmental significance, practical applications of water quality test, sampling techniques and also standards of water for various uses. well-arranged tests in Lab - dealing with some major water and waste water quality parameters of physical, chemical and biological.

Equipments: Digital Turbidity meter, Digital pH meter, Conductivity meter, Flame photometer, UV / Visible Spectrophotometer, Controlled Stirring Apparatus for Jar Tests, BOD & COD Analyser, Auto Clave for sterilization, Laminar Flow Cabinet.

COMPUTER AIDED BUILDING DRAWING LABORATORY
This lab is to acquire hands on experience in design and to prepare building Plan, Elevation, Section and structural drawings for concrete / steel structures normally encountered in Civil Engineering practice.

SYSTEMS: ACER Power Service PCs, ACER Veriton PCs, Dell Vostro PCs, HP Laser Jet A3 Size Printer, HP AKIN A4 Size Printer, Scanner & Copier, AUTO CAD-3D Modeling, Ansys, & Solid Works software.

 SCE

Saranathan College of Engineering was founded by “Vidya Seva Ratnam”, “Guru Seva Mani” Auditor Sri. K. Santhanam in the year 1998. It was so named in respectful memory of his Guru Principal Saranathan, the greatest ever Principal of National College. It is a self financing college approved by AICTE and affiliated to Anna University, Chennai.
