Labview Academy School

Why get CLAD Certified?


  • The Certified LabVIEW Associate Developer (CLAD) Certification indicates a broad working knowledge of the LabVIEW environment, a basic understanding of coding and documentation best practices, and the ability to read and interpret existing code

  • National Instruments (NI) offers the Certified LabVIEW Associate Developer (CLAD) exam using either LabVIEW or LabVIEW NXG

  • This is an international certification course offered by NI who has developed automated test and automated measurement systems that help engineers solve the worldâ€™s toughest challenges for more than 40 years

  • The pass percentage to get CLAD certified is 70% and above, meaning the certified students possess immense knowledge in LabVIEW

  • The possession of CLAD certification would give the person a competitive advantage over other candidates within the approach of recruitment

  • CLAD exams, which mainly focuses on labVIEW, is one of the most widely used technology in various Defense, Aerospace, Manufacturing, Product Design and Automation industries

  • CLAD certification is valid for 2 years from date issued

Objectives:


  • Conducting Lab VIEW training based on curriculum laboratory experiments for all the semesters

  • Hardware technical training to be provided for NIYANTRA participants

  • Conducting CLAD refresher classes

  • Conducting workshops and Seminars on Lab VIEW for the staff and students.
